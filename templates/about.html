The Mandate

"Can✝us - UCM is a non-profit effort dedicated to the free distribution of sacred music. We believe that financial barriers should never impede the beauty of the liturgy."

This project operates as a **Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)** initiative. We provide high-quality musical typesetting, audio rehearsal files, and MIDI transcriptions at no cost to choir directors and parishes worldwide.

As a non-profit initiative, our operations are powered by the generosity of contributors and partners. This collective investment allows us to keep the ledger open and accessible to all, ensuring that financial constraints never hinder liturgical excellence.

Collaborative Management

The UCM Archive is not a static library; It is a living collection managed by a global network of **Volunteer Collaborators**. These experts ensure the musical and theological accuracy of every entry.

  • 01.

    Choirmasters

    Field-testing arrangements and providing audio references for rehearsals.

  • 02.

    Transcriptionists

    Digitizing hand-written folios into modern, legible PDF formats.

  • 03.

    Liturgists

    Verifying the correct seasonal categorization for every piece of music.
